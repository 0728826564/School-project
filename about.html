Kenya Power and Lighting Company

About Us – Kenya Power and Lighting Company.
Kenya Power and Lighting Company is the national company responsible for distributing and selling electricity in Kenya. The
company supplies power to homes, businesses, and industries across the country, helping support economic growth and daily life.

Vision: To be Kenya’s energy solutions provider of choice.
Mission: Powering people for better lives through reliable electricity services.
Services:
Electricity distribution
New power connections
Customer billing and support
Maintenance of the power network
Ownership: Majority owned by the Government of Kenya.
